How are KevCoins Laid out?
ver: KevCoin version 1
bits: Number of bits in the hashed code. (Certain bits convert to certain values of KevCoin)
date: The time that the coin was mined, in the format YYMMDD[hhmm[ss].
sig: Your chosen signature to validate the coin in your name (if you have a coin write in the database transaction and the coin name)
key: Your key is a sort of pin to verify its you
rand: String of random characters, encoded in base-64 format.
counter: Binary counter, encoded in base-64 format.
